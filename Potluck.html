Potluck

UX

Interaction Design

Food always tastes better when shared with others! I built a mobile application that helps users find recipes both new and old alike, and encourages them to share them with friends and family.

This project was part of a design exercise I recieved during the application process at Yelp. My process involved research and problem-framing, developing user personas, wireframes, mockups, and high-fidelity prototypes.
