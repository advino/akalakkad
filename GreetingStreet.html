Case Study: Greeting Street

UX

Data-Driven Design

Physical Computing

Last fall I participated in a studio sponsored by Tellart, an Experience Design studio based in Providence, RI. I developed a set of digital kiosks that facilitated micro-interactions between city commuters in order to help develop empathetic city environments.

My process included field reserach, problem framing, paper/low-fidelity prototyping, electronic hardware design, and user testing.
