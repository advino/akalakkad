Interaction Design

An exercise in Behavior-Change Design, I built a mobile assistant that helps users make both healthier and financially smarter choices in supermarkets.

My process involved initial research and problem-framing, developing user personas, wireframes, mockups, and high-fidelity prototypes. My assumptions and hypothesis' were then tested through on-site user testing.
