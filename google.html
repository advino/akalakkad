Over the summer of 2018 I had the incredible fortune work at Google AI as a UX Intern. As an intern, I worked on developing compelling use-cases for AIY Projects, an experimental prototyping platorm for designing AI-centered products.

I conducted user research and problem framing, developed interaction patterns and design guidlines for AIY, and implemented my designs in Python alongside intelligence models such as ImageNet.

While I can't showcase the details just yet, I am happy to say that my project will be open-sourced on the AIY website this fall! If you have more questions, feel free to email me!
