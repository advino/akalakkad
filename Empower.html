Case Study: Empower

UX

Interaction Design

Empower is a start-up based in San Francisco. Their main goal is to help users make responsible financial decisions while building personal wealth. I was given the opportunity to iterate on a possible investment feature within the application, aimed at helping users understand the value of investing and how they might begin the process.

My process involved research and problem-framing, developing user personas, wireframes, mockups, and high-fidelity prototypes. Addtionally I worked with developers on the team to perform feasibility tests and reiterate on user interfaces and flows
